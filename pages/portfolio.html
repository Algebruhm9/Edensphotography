Behind the scenes

After I finish capturing photos, I go home and begin my editing process. Depending on how many pictures I took that day it can take me hours or an entire evening to pick and reject photos up to finishing processing them and posting them online. I start off by sorting all of the pictures in Lightroom and choosing out the good ones from the bad ones. I usually end up with about 1/3rd of all the pictures I took which makes it easier on the time spent editing these photos. Once all the photos have been selected I begin examining all the photos to see what style or editing process I can do to each of them. I like to keep a simple and common styling process for my photos when editing. I like to add a little more saturation, emphasize on the contrast, add a vignette to the image and overall make it look completely different from the original so I generally like to make it a look a bit dramatic.
